**MAP ART / GEO-ART FEATURE – GRAFFITI KINGS APP**

**Concept:**
Users create strategic Google Maps pins for sticker hits, painted walls, drops, projections, and other street interventions. SPARK helps plan the pins so that when viewed as a completed route/walk, the path forms the **outline of an image** (bunny, dog, cat, brand logo, symbol, word, icon, etc.).

This turns a series of street drops into a **treasure map / walk tour** that fans can follow, geotag, and complete — creating a powerful, shareable, and press-worthy experience.

---

### **How It Works**

**Step 1: Planning (SPARK + User)**
- User tells SPARK what image/shape they want to create (bunny, dog, cat, brand name, heart, fist, crown, etc.)
- SPARK suggests optimal pin locations that form the desired outline when connected as a walking route
- SPARK considers:
  - High-visibility / high-traffic spots
  - Legal grey areas and low-risk zones
  - Cultural relevance to the message
  - Walkability and safety
  - Photogenic locations for documentation

**Step 2: Execution**
- User drops at each planned pin location (stickers, wheatpaste, paint, projection, etc.)
- User documents each drop (photos, videos, geotags)
- SPARK helps track progress and suggests order/timing

**Step 3: Completion & Reveal**
- Once all pins are complete, the full route forms the desired image when viewed on Google Maps (as a walking path)
- SPARK generates:
  - A beautiful map screenshot with the outline highlighted
  - A "treasure map" style graphic for fans
  - Step-by-step walking instructions
  - Geotag challenges for fans to complete

**Step 4: Fan Engagement**
- Fans follow the map, visit each location, and geotag their visit
- Fans can share their progress, photos, and completion
- Creates organic spread and community participation

**Step 5: Press & Marketing**
- The finished map becomes a unique, visual press release asset
- News sites love fresh, visual, shareable content
- SPARK helps generate unique press releases for multiple outlets

---

**1. Unique Visual Content**

**2. Shareable & Viral**

**3. Press-Worthy Story**
- “Local artist creates giant bunny map across the city using street art”

**4. Brand / Message Integration**
- The shape can be a brand logo, symbol, word, or powerful icon
- Turns street art into a branded experience

**5. Long-Term Asset**
- The map can live on as a permanent digital asset

- Creates ongoing engagement and storytelling

### **SPARK Coordination Logic for This Feature**

**When user wants to create a Map Art piece:**

1. **Creative Suite + Trait System** – Suggest shapes, symbols, and concepts that fit the user’s message and brand
2. **Campaign OS + Trait System** – Plan the 30-day (or multi-week) execution timeline
3. **Risk Framework** – Ensure all pin locations are legal/safe or low-risk with clear warnings
4. **Viral Patterns + Video Strategy** – Plan documentation for maximum spread (before/after, process, treasure map reveal)
5. **Monetization Tools** – Suggest how to turn the experience into prints, merch, limited editions, or brand collabs
6. **Lore Engine** – Connect the map to a larger story or HODL WARRIORS faction if relevant
7. **Emotional Intelligence** – Match the user’s energy and help them stay motivated through the multi-week project
8. **User Context & Memory** – Reference past drops and locations for continuity
9. **Waste Management** – Plan for potential setbacks and how to turn them into content

**SPARK Output:**
A complete, step-by-step plan that includes:
- The final map outline (visual)
- Pin-by-pin execution plan with timing and technique
- Documentation strategy
- Press release templates
- Fan engagement plan
- Risk assessment for each location

### **Technical Implementation**

**Google Maps Integration:**
- User creates pins in Google Maps (or the app generates them)
- SPARK suggests optimal pin placement to form the desired shape
- App can export the route as a shareable link or image
- Fans can follow the route in Google Maps and check off locations as they complete them

**App Features:**
- Map Art planner tool (drag pins to form shapes)
- Auto-suggest pin locations based on desired shape + street art best practices
- Progress tracker for multi-week projects
- One-tap press release generator (customized for different outlets)
- Fan completion tracker and leaderboard (optional)

**Privacy & Safety:**
- All location data is user-controlled
- Users can make maps public or private
- Risk assessment is always included
- No location data is sold or shared without explicit consent
