**GRAFFITI KINGS – MONETIZATION PLAYBOOK**

**Purpose:**
This document outlines clear, realistic ways users can make money with GRAFFITI KINGS — from day one through long-term empire building. The goal is to give users multiple paths to revenue while staying true to the street-first philosophy.

---

### **Tiered Monetization Approach**

**Phase 1: Quick Wins (First 30 Days)**
- Limited edition prints of your drops
- Simple merch (stickers, posters, tees) sold via your own channels
- Commissioned work for local businesses or brands
- Patreon or Ko-fi for early supporters

**Phase 2: Building Income (30-90 Days)**
- Rate card for street art services (drops, campaigns, Map Art pieces)
- Brand collaborations and sponsorships
- Digital products (Notion templates, campaign planners, press release packs)
- Affiliate income from tools you recommend

**Phase 3: Scaling (3-12 Months)**
- Web3 revenue (NFT drops, token-gated content, faction membership)
- Physical exhibitions and gallery shows
- Speaking / workshops / teaching
- Long-term brand partnerships
- Own product line (apparel, home goods, limited art objects)

---

### **Specific Revenue Streams (Ranked by Accessibility)**

**1. Physical Prints & Merch (Easiest to Start)**
- Print your best drops as limited editions
- Sell via Instagram, your own site, or local pop-ups
- Use SPARK to generate product descriptions and pricing

**2. Commissioned Street Work**
- Create a simple rate card (SPARK can help)
- Pitch local businesses, bars, record labels, clothing brands
- Start with small jobs (£300-£800) and scale up

**3. Digital Products**
- Sell your campaign templates, press release packs, or Map Art planning tools
- Create once, sell infinitely
- SPARK can help you package and price these

**4. Brand Collaborations**
- Once you have a portfolio and audience, approach brands
- SPARK can help write professional outreach emails and proposals

**5. Web3 Revenue (Higher Upside, Higher Effort)**
- Drop limited NFTs tied to physical pieces or Map Art
- Create token-gated content or experiences
- Build a faction with paid membership perks
- Revenue share from community-created content

**6. Long-Term Assets**
- Build a recognizable personal brand
- License your style or characters
- Create recurring revenue through subscriptions or memberships
