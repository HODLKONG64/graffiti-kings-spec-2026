**GRAFFITI KINGS APP – ONBOARDING FLOW (DETAILED)**

**Purpose:**
This document outlines the complete onboarding experience for new users. The goal is to make the first 5-10 minutes magical, clear, and motivating — so users immediately understand the value and feel excited to start.

---

### **Onboarding Goals**

1. Make users feel **seen and understood** within the first 60 seconds
2. Clearly explain the **SPARK philosophy** (you don’t need power to start)
3. Show **immediate value** (give them something useful in under 5 minutes)
4. Set the right **expectations** for each tier (LITE → FULL → MEGA+)
5. Get them to their **first small win** as fast as possible

---

### **Step-by-Step Onboarding Flow**

**Step 0: Pre-Install (App Store / Website)**
- Tagline: “SPARK. Plug it in. Watch it take off.”
- Short description: “The only AI that doesn’t need power to start. Just a SPARK.”
- Visual: Strong street art imagery + rocketship metaphor

**Step 1: Welcome Screen (First 10 seconds)**
- Big bold text: “Welcome to GRAFFITI KINGS.”
- Subtext: “You don’t need power to start. You just need a SPARK.”
- One big button: “Let’s Begin”
- Small text at bottom: “100% offline. No signup required.”

**Step 2: Quick Philosophy (30 seconds)**
- Short animated explanation:
  - Most AI apps need big servers and expensive APIs.
  - We start with just a SPARK.
  - Plug it in later for more power.
- Visual: Simple animation showing a spark turning into a rocket
- Button: “Got it”

**Step 3: Energy & Goal Check (60 seconds)**
- Friendly question: “How are you feeling right now?” (Tired / Motivated / Curious / Overwhelmed / Excited)
- Second question: “What do you want to create?” (Street drops / Build audience / Make money / Start a movement / Just explore)
- SPARK responds with empathy + sets the right tone for their state

**Step 4: Instant Value (First Small Win – 2-3 minutes)**
- SPARK immediately generates a **tiny 7-day starter plan** based on their answers
- Example: “Here’s a simple 7-day plan to get your first 3 drops done with almost zero risk.”
- One-tap to accept or customize
- This gives them **immediate momentum** and proof the app works

**Step 5: Tier Explanation (Optional but Recommended)**
- Quick, non-pushy explanation of the three tiers:
  - LITE (what you have now — already powerful)
  - FULL (connect your API for rocket fuel)
  - MEGA+ (full local brain when you’re ready)
- Clear message: “You can stay in LITE forever and still build real things. Upgrade when you want more power.”

**Step 6: First Action + Celebration**
- After they accept their first plan, SPARK celebrates:
  “Nice. You just took your first step. Most people never even start. You’re already ahead.”
- Offers to walk them through Day 1 or let them explore on their own

**Step 7: Optional Profile Setup**
- Light profile questions (location, main goal, experience level, risk tolerance)
- This feeds the User Context & Memory system immediately
- Can be skipped and completed later

---

### **SPARK Onboarding Personality**

- Warm, encouraging, slightly rebellious
- Never salesy or pushy about upgrading
- Celebrates small wins enthusiastically
- Makes the user feel capable and supported from minute one
- Uses the user’s chosen SPARK personality (Raw Street / Chill Mentor / Sarcastic Legend) from the start

---

### **Success Metrics for Onboarding**

- % of users who complete the first 7-day plan
- % of users who return within 48 hours
- Average time to first drop
- User sentiment in first-week feedback
- Upgrade rate from LITE to FULL within 30 days

---

**Final Truth:**
The first 5 minutes decide whether someone becomes a lifelong user or deletes the app. Our onboarding must feel like meeting a wise, slightly chaotic street-smart friend who believes in you more than you believe in yourself. That’s the GRAFFITI KINGS way.
