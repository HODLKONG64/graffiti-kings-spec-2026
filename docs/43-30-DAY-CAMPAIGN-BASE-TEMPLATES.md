**30-DAY CAMPAIGN BASE TEMPLATES – For Trait System Mixing**

**Purpose:**
These are the core base templates the Campaign Trait System pulls from and mixes. They give the agent solid starting points to create unique 30-day plans without needing external AI.

---

### **Template 1: The Raw Drop (Beginner / Lazy Mode)**

**Goal:** Get visible fast with minimal effort and low risk.

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 5-7 simple wheatpaste or sticker designs
- Drop in 3-5 high-traffic but low-risk locations
- Use reverse graffiti or chalk for legal safety

**Week 3-4:**
- Document everything (before/after photos)
- Post 2-3 times per week with consistent captions
- Track which locations perform best

**Traits to Mix:** Location, Technique (low risk), Tone (simple & direct)

---

### **Template 2: The Story Drop (Personal Brand Building)**

**Goal:** Build emotional connection and long-term followers.

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 3-4 pieces that tell parts of your personal story
- Use wheatpaste + hand-written elements for authenticity
- Drop in emotionally relevant locations (childhood areas, meaningful spots)

**Week 3-4:**
- Share the full story across posts
- Engage with every comment
- Offer limited prints or behind-the-scenes content

**Traits to Mix:** Tone (emotional/poetic), Location (personal), Monetization (prints + story)

---

### **Template 3: The Political / Cause Drop (Activism Mode)**

**Goal:** Create impact and start conversations.

**Week 1-2:**
- Research current local issue
- Create 4-6 sharp, clear messages
- Use high-visibility but smart locations
- Prepare legal scripts and media contacts in advance

**Week 3-4:**
- Document reactions and media coverage
- Turn responses into follow-up content
- Build community around the issue

**Traits to Mix:** Tone (defiant/angry), Risk Level (medium-high), Audience (activists + locals)

---

### **Template 4: The Business / Brand Drop (Monetization Focus)**

**Goal:** Drive real business results (foot traffic, clients, sales).

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 5-7 designs that promote your product/service
- Use QR codes that track performance
- Target locations near your ideal customers

**Week 3-4:**
- Analyze which drops converted best
- Create follow-up content based on results
- Offer special deals for people who found you through the drops

**Traits to Mix:** Monetization (direct sales), Audience (customers), Technique (QR + tracking)

---

### **Template 5: The Mystery / Lore Drop (Engagement Focus)**

**Goal:** Create buzz and long-term following through storytelling.

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 3-4 mysterious pieces that hint at a larger story
- Drop in a sequence that builds curiosity
- Use consistent symbols or characters

**Week 3-4:**
- Reveal more of the story gradually
- Encourage people to find all pieces
- Turn it into a collectible or community experience

**Traits to Mix:** Tone (mysterious), Visual Style (cinematic), Lore integration

---

### **How the Trait System Uses These Templates**

The agent takes one base template and mixes in different traits (location, tone, technique, risk level, etc.) to create a unique 30-day plan for each user.

This gives near-infinite variety while staying grounded in proven campaign structures.

**Agent Rule:**
Always start with one of these 5 base templates, then apply trait mixing. Never create a 30-day plan from scratch without a base structure.
