**30-DAY CAMPAIGN BASE TEMPLATES – EXPANDED VERSION**

**Purpose:**
These are the core base templates the Campaign Trait System pulls from and mixes. They give the agent solid, proven starting points to create unique 30-day plans without needing external AI.

---

### **Template 1: The Raw Drop (Beginner / Lazy Mode)**

**Goal:** Get visible fast with minimal effort and low risk.

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 5-7 simple wheatpaste or sticker designs (keep them bold and simple)
- Drop in 3-5 high-traffic but low-risk locations (high streets at night, quiet alleys, underpasses)
- Use reverse graffiti or chalk for legal safety where possible
- Document everything with before/after photos

**Week 3-4:**
- Post 2-3 times per week with consistent captions and hashtags
- Track which locations and designs perform best
- Engage with every comment and message
- Offer a simple way for people to support (link in bio, small prints, or just "follow for more")

**Best For:** Beginners, low energy, low risk tolerance, building initial audience
**Traits to Mix:** Location (safe), Technique (low risk), Tone (simple & direct), Viral Hook (before/after)

---

### **Template 2: The Story Drop (Personal Brand Building)**

**Goal:** Build emotional connection and long-term followers.

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 3-4 pieces that tell parts of your personal story (struggle, healing, identity, loss, triumph)
- Use wheatpaste + hand-written elements or raw phone photos for authenticity
- Drop in emotionally relevant locations (childhood areas, meaningful spots, places that represent your journey)
- Film the process and your feelings about it

**Week 3-4:**
- Share the full story across posts and a longer video
- Engage deeply with every comment (this is where connection happens)
- Offer limited prints, behind-the-scenes access, or a simple way to support
- Turn comments and messages into follow-up content

**Best For:** Building loyal audience, emotional connection, long-term brand
**Traits to Mix:** Tone (emotional/poetic), Location (personal), Documentation (story format + mini doc), Viral Hook (emotion + relatability)

---

### **Template 3: The Political / Cause Drop (Activism Mode)**

**Goal:** Create real impact and start conversations.

**Week 1-2:**
- Research current local or national issue that matters to you
- Create 4-6 sharp, clear, powerful messages
- Use high-visibility but smart locations (with escape routes and legal awareness)
- Prepare legal scripts, media contacts, and FOI templates in advance
- Film the drop and any reactions

**Week 3-4:**
- Document reactions, media coverage, and community response
- Turn responses into follow-up content and threads
- Build community around the issue (group chat, next steps, calls to action)
- Analyze what worked and what didn’t for future campaigns

**Best For:** Creating impact, starting conversations, building movement
**Traits to Mix:** Tone (defiant/angry), Risk Level (medium-high), Audience (activists + locals), Viral Hook (controversy + timeliness)

---

### **Template 4: The Business / Brand Drop (Monetization Focus)**

**Goal:** Drive real business results (foot traffic, clients, sales, leads).

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 5-7 designs that clearly promote your product/service/brand
- Use QR codes that track performance by location
- Target locations near your ideal customers (near universities, offices, events, etc.)
- Make the message benefit-focused (what’s in it for them)

**Week 3-4:**
- Analyze which drops converted best (which locations, which designs, which times)
- Create follow-up content based on results
- Offer special deals or exclusives for people who found you through the drops
- Build a simple rate card and outreach list for similar businesses

**Best For:** Small businesses, freelancers, creators who want to make money
**Traits to Mix:** Monetization (direct sales + client outreach), Audience (customers), Technique (QR + tracking), Viral Hook (results + proof)

---

### **Template 5: The Mystery / Lore Drop (Engagement Focus)**

**Goal:** Create buzz, curiosity, and long-term following through storytelling.

**Week 1-2:**
- Create 3-4 mysterious pieces that hint at a larger story or universe
- Drop in a sequence that builds curiosity (different locations, connected symbols)
- Use consistent characters, symbols, or phrases
- Film the process and the feeling of creating something bigger

**Week 3-4:**
- Reveal more of the story gradually across posts and videos
- Encourage people to find all pieces and piece the story together
- Turn it into a collectible experience, community puzzle, or ongoing series
- Invite people to contribute ideas or become part of the story

**Best For:** Building long-term engagement, lore, community, repeat viewers
**Traits to Mix:** Tone (mysterious), Visual Style (cinematic), Lore integration, Viral Hook (mystery + story)

---

**How the Trait System Uses These Templates:**
The agent takes one base template and mixes in different traits (location, tone, technique, risk level, audience, monetization, viral hook, etc.) to create a unique 30-day plan for each user.

This gives near-infinite variety while staying grounded in proven campaign structures.

**Agent Rule:**
Always start with one of these 5 base templates, then apply trait mixing. Never create a 30-day plan from scratch without a base structure. This keeps plans realistic, executable, and effective.
